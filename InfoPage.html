Our Purpose

We created this program because an issue we have noticed among our age group is that many teenagers have unhealthy coping mechanisms when dealing with stress, as well as poor time management that causes stress in the first place. In order to assist with this problem, our website offers two fundamental pieces. The first being a scheduling section where people can note any important to-doâ€™s they have for various days, so nothing important is forgotten about. The second component is more mental health focused. Journaling is a proven aid that has helped with combating mental health issues. People can journal their thoughts, feelings, and ideas each day in the journaling log, and if they are at a loss for ideas, there are also a wide variety of prompts provided to write about. Along with the journal we also have a mood log, where people can input how they are feeling on a given day with a simple emoji. Based on this, they will be provided a quote that hopefully encourages them and brightens their day a bit more.
